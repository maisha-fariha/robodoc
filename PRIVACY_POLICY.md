# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RoboDoc ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the RoboDoc mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

RoboDoc may collect the following categories of information:

- Account information (such as email address and authentication identifiers) when you sign in or create an account.
- Health and assessment information you enter in the app (for example symptoms, pain level, and follow-up responses).
- Technical information needed to operate the app (such as device/app diagnostics and basic usage logs).
- Profile information you choose to provide (for example age, selected profile fields, and profile image path/asset data).

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide symptom assessment and AI-assisted triage-style responses.
- Generate dynamic follow-up questions and assessment summaries.
- Save account/session state and improve user experience.
- Maintain app security, troubleshoot issues, and improve app quality.

## 3. AI Processing

RoboDoc uses AI services to generate follow-up questions and assessment summaries based on your inputs.

- Your assessment inputs may be sent to an AI provider for processing.
- AI output is informational only and does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
- Do not use RoboDoc for emergency decision-making. In emergencies, contact local emergency services immediately.

## 4. Authentication and Storage

- RoboDoc uses Firebase Authentication for account sign-in features.
- Some app data may be stored locally on your device (for example offline/session-related data) to support app functionality.
- We recommend protecting your device with passcode/biometric lock to reduce unauthorized local access.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data only with service providers required to run app features, such as:

- Authentication provider (Firebase)
- AI processing provider (for AI response generation)

These providers process data according to their own terms and policies.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to:

- Provide app functionality
- Meet legal obligations
- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Locally stored data may remain on your device until you remove app data or uninstall the app.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 8. Children's Privacy

RoboDoc is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect data from children without required consent.

## 9. Your Choices

Depending on your region and applicable law, you may have rights to:

- Access or correct your personal information
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent where processing is consent-based

To exercise rights, contact us using the details below.

## 10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including where service providers operate infrastructure.

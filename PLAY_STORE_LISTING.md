## Full description (en-GB)

```
RoboDoc is a mobile app for a structured, seven-step wellness questionnaire. It is for general information only — not a medical diagnosis, prescription, or emergency service. If you need urgent care, use your local emergency number.

What happens in the assessment
• Step 1: your age and gender selection (male, female, or self-identify).
• Step 2: describe your symptoms in your own words, with optional quick picks (for example fever, headache, cough, palpitations, fatigue).
• Step 3: how long you have felt this way, including preset ranges and a day slider.
• Step 4: symptom severity (including pain intensity and related context you provide in the flow).
• Steps 5–7: three follow-up questions generated by AI from your earlier answers. These steps need a working internet connection. If AI generation is unavailable, the app still completes an assessment using built-in logic and shows a results screen.

Results screen
• A headline, a “primary indication” label, a short narrative summary, a confidence percentage, an ICD-10-style reference label for context, a LOW / MOD / HIGH risk level with supporting text, temperature / heart rate / SpO2-style figures shown alongside your summary (these are produced by the app from your answers — not from a medical device), and care-style suggestion cards.

Afterwards
• Open History to see past assessments you have saved on your device (the app keeps up to the last 50).
• Sign in with email and password or with Google where offered, using Firebase Authentication.
• Home, assessment, history, and profile areas are available from the main navigation.

Privacy and account deletion URLs are on this store listing.

Support: info@encoderit.com
```

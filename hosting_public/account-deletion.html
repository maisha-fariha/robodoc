RoboDoc Account and Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your RoboDoc account and associated data, please submit a request by email.

How to request deletion

  1. Email saadnanullah@gmail.com from the same email you used in RoboDoc.
  2. Use subject: Delete RoboDoc Account.
  3. Include your app account email address in the message body.

What we delete

  • Account authentication records associated with your email.
  • Assessment and profile data associated with your account, where applicable.

Timeline

We process verified deletion requests within 7 business days.
